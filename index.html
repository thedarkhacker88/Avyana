About Us

Avyana Software Solutions provides advanced School ERP Software to streamline school management, covering student records, fee collection, attendance, exams, transport, and more. Our secure, user-friendly, and customizable solutions empower educational institutions with seamless automation.

We specialize in developing custom software solutions for schools, finance, and business management. Our innovative and scalable software enhances efficiency, automation, and seamless operations.

Student Management

Our Student Management Module streamlines admissions, attendance, and academics. It ensures real-time tracking and seamless school-parent communication.

Accounts Management

Our Accounts Management System automates fee collection, expenses, and financial reporting. It ensures accuracy, transparency, and hassle-free transactions for schools.

HRMS & Payroll

Our HRMS & Payroll System manages staff records, attendance, leaves, and salary processing with ease. It ensures timely payouts, compliance, and efficient HR operations.

Customer Support.

We offer multi-channel support through phone, email, and chat to ensure smooth software operation. Our experts are well-trained to resolve technical issues, guide users, and provide training when required. We prioritize quick response times and long-term client satisfaction.

With Avyana, you’re never alone—we’re with you at every step of your journey.
